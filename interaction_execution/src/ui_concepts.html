How to Use WordleBot
Switching Views
Use the view selector to switch between Sim View, MoveIt View, and Camera View. In Camera View, toggle between raw footage and computer vision modes.
Voice Control
Select Mode B to enable voice input. Click Record and speak your Wordle guess.
Safety Controls
Use START to begin a mission, STOP to halt immediately, RESUME to continue from a pause, and HOME to return to starting position.
Wordle Game
Mode A: Robot guesses. Mode B: You guess via voice or typing.
